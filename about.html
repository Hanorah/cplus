About CGMI  


Church Plus is deeply rooted in the heritage of Church of God Mission International (CGMi), a global Pentecostal ministry initiated by Archbishop Benson Idahosa, affectionately called Papa. CGMi originated as Calvary Fellowship, a prayer ministry led by Pastor Benson Idahosa in 1962. It transitioned into an independent ministry by 1968, officially becoming Church of God Mission International Inc. (CGMi Inc.) in 1974. Evangelism has always been CGMi's primary focus, reflected in its motto: "Evangelism our Supreme Task." Papa's evangelistic endeavors resulted in the establishment of numerous churches, alongside various ministries, schools, and medical facilities. These initiatives, including campus ministries, youth fellowships, schools, and medical centers, aimed to nurture Christians who are spiritually, mentally, and physically whole. Following Archbishop Benson Idahosa's passing in 1998, Archbishop Margaret E. Benson-Idahosa has upheld CGMi's vision as the presiding Bishop.


About Church Plus

Welcome to Church Plus, a beacon of hope and transformation in our community. Founded in 2008 by Rev. Emmanuel Ubamadu, our church was born from a profound vision to break free from the constraints of religious tradition and embrace the liberating message of grace. In 2020, Rev. Dr. Carl Umakhihe was entrusted with the continuation of this vision, propelling us forward into a new era of growth and impact. At Church Plus, we believe in the power of God's word to change lives and shape destinies. We are not just a congregation; we are a community of believers who are continually transformed by the teachings of Jesus Christ. As we are changed by His word, we are empowered to change our world. Join us on this journey of faith, growth, and service. Together, we can make a difference and spread the message of God's love and grace to all.

Who we're looking for

At Church Plus, we welcome everyone with open arms, whether you're taking your first steps in faith or have been on a lifelong spiritual journey.

We believe in creating a warm and homely atmosphere where individuals from all walks of life can come together to grow spiritually, find support, and build meaningful connections. No matter where you are in your journey, you'll find a place to belong here at Church Plus.

Join us as we explore the richness of faith, share in fellowship, and journey together towards a deeper understanding of God's love.
